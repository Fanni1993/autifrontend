3 things you can do:

  1. Be nice! Hang out with them!

    2. People with autism aren't aliens. Just treat them like everyone else.
    The more you hang out with somebody who has ASD the less uncomfortable you feel around them.
    Ask them about their hobbies, interests. You will be surprised!

  2. Don't overreact if they do something "funny".

    3. Many autistic people has a problem called Sensory Integration Dsyfunction. They can be hypersensitive about smells,
    noises, stuff like that. This is why sometimes they act weird

  3. Learn about autism. Teach about it.

    4. Read books or online media. Visit events about autism. Check out World Autism Awareness 2018
